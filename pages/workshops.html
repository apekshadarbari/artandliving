Therapeutic Arts Workshops

Refill Your Cup

A room — online, for an hour or an afternoon — where you get to exist as you are. Where your hands make something with no brief and no grade and no right answer. Where the only instruction is to follow what you already know.

What to expect

A session that feels
like permission

No art experience needed. No right way to show up. Whatever materials you have at home are the right materials. You make alongside me, camera on or off, sharing only what you want to share.


The space is small and intentional — never more than 11–12 women online. You will be witnessed. You will not be fixed.

  • 01

    Landing meditation

    A short guided moment to leave the day behind and arrive in your body. Not elaborate — just enough to shift gears.

  • 02

    The warm-up

    A gentle reframe to ease into making mode. A little of what the science says about creativity and the nervous system.

  • 03

    The making

    One gentle prompt. Whatever materials you have. Apeksha makes alongside you. No watching, no evaluating. Just presence.

  • 04

    Sharing circle

    Not "what did you make" but "what did you notice." Nothing required. Nobody called on.

  • 05

    Soft close

    An invitation, never a push. You leave when you're ready.
