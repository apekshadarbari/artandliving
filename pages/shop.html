Wild Wisps

These are some of my favourite pieces from my first 100 paintings — the beginning of a new path in my journey. I named this first release Wild Wisps because that is how this expedition started, with little itches to create. Little did I know then that this would become a ritual, and eventually, a pilgrimage.

I'm glad you are here. Enjoy the art.
